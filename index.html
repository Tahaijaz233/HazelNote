How to Get YouTube Transcript

  1. 1. Open your YouTube video in a new tab
  2. 2. Click the "..." (more) button below the video
  3. 3. Select "Show Transcript" from the menu
  4. 4. Click the transcript text, press Ctrl+A (or Cmd+A on Mac) to select all
  5. 5. Copy and paste it into the box below

💡 Alternative: If the video doesn't have built-in transcripts, use youtube-transcript.io - a free tool that generates transcripts for any YouTube video.
